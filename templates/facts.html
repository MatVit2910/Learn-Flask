Fun Facts About Ducks!

  • Ducks can sleep with one eye open to stay alert for predators.
  • There are over 120 different species of ducks around the world.
  • Male ducks are called drakes, females are hens, and babies are ducklings.
  • Ducks have waterproof feathers thanks to a special oil they produce.
  • Some duck species migrate thousands of miles every year.
  • Ducks have a wide field of vision — almost 340 degrees!
  • They can swim, fly, and walk on land — triple threat!
  • Ducks have been domesticated for more than 500 years.
  • Their quack doesn’t echo — and no one knows why (well, it's a myth, but it's fun!).
  • Ducks use their bills to filter food from water and mud.
